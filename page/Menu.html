Top Nature Destinations

1. Munnar, Kerala

Famous for its rolling tea gardens and misty mountains.

2. Jim Corbett National Park, Uttarakhand

The oldest national park in India, perfect for tiger spotting.

3. Valley of Flowers, Uttarakhand

A high-altitude Himalayan valley known for its meadows of endemic alpine flowers.

The largest mangrove forest in the world.
